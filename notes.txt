An HTTP endpoint:
    Is a targetable URL in the web application, 
        such as https://localhost:5001/HelloWorld.
    Combines, e.g.: 
        1. The protocol used: HTTPS.
        2. The network location of the web server, 
            including the TCP port: localhost:5001.
        3. The target URI: HelloWorld.
